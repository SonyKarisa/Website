What does KLICK do?

KLICK provides a streamlined service where you can book and hire photographers, videographers, or models for your personal creative needs. It is a digital e-commerce platform, and our headquarters are in Calgary Alberta. Since 1994, our dedicated team has worked tirelessly and diligently to make the platform seamless and easy to use. We aim to make it easy for you to find creators who can capture your special moments or be the muse of your creativity.
